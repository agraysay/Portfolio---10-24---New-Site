ABOUT ME

I grew up in the Detroit area and went to college outside of New York City, where I studied New Media. I've spent the last year as a graphic designer and screenprinter at Underground Printing, a apparel shop based in Ann Arbor. I've always been interested in coding, and spent some time building simple websites in college. I'm very excited to go deeper into front-end development to enhance and further my design skills.
